Taiwander needs your help!

Taiwander is cheering hard for Taiwan every day! Now it's racing in the YuruChara Grand Prix 2015! This the biggest competition for mascot characters in Japan, the one that made Kumamon famous. The competition is really tough, Taiwander is struggling and needs your support!
You can help Taiwander by voting on the Yuruchara Grand Prix website. It's all in Japanese, but it's very easy after you've done it once. We've written some instructions to help.

What is Taiwander?
Taiwander is lives in Japan, here is its biography. It's a volunteer with the Cheer4Taiwan organisation working to promote the beautiful island of Taiwan to Japan! Taiwander isn't connected to the government or any business. Let's watch this video to see Taiwander dancing live to its theme song,
"Taiwan the Tropical Island" (written and sung by Kenta Ebara).

Music Video Version | Taiwanese Version

Why is Taiwander struggling in the YuruChara Grand Prix?
Most people in Japan give their vote to their own local character and business characters are supported by their company. Taiwander is in the 'overseas character' category, competing against characters working for foreign governments (and 7-11!). Taiwander needs support from people in Taiwan, but even though it loves Taiwan very much it's very shy about speaking Taiwanese!

Who are you?
We're just fans who want to help Taiwander! We're not affiliated with Cheer4Taiwan or any other organisation in Japan or Taiwan.
